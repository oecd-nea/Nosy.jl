Modelling Concepts

Start with the snapshot-building concepts when you are defining a system, then optimisation, finally concepts when you need results.

  • Time: temporal structure of the model
  • Building A Snapshot: simulations, carriers, ports, nodes, components, archetypes, behaviors, joint flows, snapshots, time, and tags.
  • Optimizing: optimizing one or more snapshots
  • Querying: balances, flows, metrics, tables, and optimization objectives.
  • Exporting / Importing: exporting and importing snapshots.
